Who We Are

Western Outreach Ministries (WESO) is the evangelism and outreach department of Masinde Muliro University Christian Union (MMUST CU). We are a passionate community of believers committed to fulfilling the Great Commission by reaching the lost with the transformative message of Jesus Christ.

Since our establishment, we have been at the forefront of campus evangelism, community outreach, and discipleship training. Our ministry extends beyond the university walls into hospitals, prisons, markets, and rural communities across Western Kenya.

We believe that every soul matters to God, and we are dedicated to ensuring that no one in our reach goes without hearing the gospel. Through the power of the Holy Spirit, we have witnessed thousands of lives transformed and communities impacted for Christ.
