Our Ministry Wings

WESO operates through specialized teams, each focusing on unique outreach fields. Join the wing that aligns with your calling and passion for souls!

Evangelism Team

The core of our ministry. This team leads campus evangelism, street outreach, door-to-door witnessing, and mass crusades. If you're passionate about sharing the gospel boldly, this is your place.

Learn More

Media Team

Capturing and sharing the ministry's impact through photography, videography, graphic design, and social media. Help us tell the stories of transformed lives to inspire and reach more souls.

Learn More

Hospital Ministry

Bringing hope and healing to the sick and their families. We visit hospitals regularly to pray for patients, share testimonies, and minister God's comfort to those in their most vulnerable moments.

Learn More

Prison Ministry

Taking the message of forgiveness and restoration behind bars. We minister to inmates, provide spiritual counseling, distribute Bibles, and follow up on those who accept Christ in prison facilities.

Learn More

Market Outreach

Meeting people where they work and trade. Our teams visit local markets, sharing the gospel with traders, customers, and shoppers. We've seen tremendous salvations in these bustling centers of commerce.

Learn More

Follow-up & Discipleship

Nurturing new believers to maturity in Christ. This team ensures that those who accept Christ are integrated into fellowship, receive biblical teaching, and grow in their faith journey.

Learn More
